STORIES


Stories exist as one of the most powerful ways to motivate others to make a change in their life. Stories alone have the power to instruct and inform in a single medium, and as such are a powerful tool for communicating complex ideas to large groups of people. If you feel like you have a story about cancer that deserves to be shared with the world, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. We would love to hear what you have to say, and a number of the responses that we recieve can and will be featured on this page in an effort to garner public attention about the human toll that cancer takes on our planet.
